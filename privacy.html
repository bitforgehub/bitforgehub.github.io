Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Bitforge Software Solutions, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website or services, including API integrations, customizable plugins, and addon management tools. We are committed to transparency and compliance with applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  1. Personal Information: Details you provide, such as name, email address, and other information submitted via contact forms or service interactions.
  2. Usage Data: Non-personal information about your website interactions, including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and session duration, collected through cookies and analytics tools.
  3. Service-Related Data: Information provided during the use of our software, such as configuration settings or preferences, to optimize your experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  1. Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and enhance our services, including responding to inquiries and delivering tailored solutions.
  2. Communication: To send updates, promotions, or information about new features related to our products.
  3. Analytics: To analyze website and service usage to improve performance and user experience.
  4. Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and interests.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in these cases:

  1. Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in delivering our services (e.g., hosting or analytics providers) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  2. Legal Requirements: When required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal process.
  3. Safety and Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Bitforge, our users, or the public.

5. Data Security

We employ robust security measures:

  1. Encryption: To protect data during transmission and storage.
  2. Access Controls: To limit access to authorized personnel only.
  3. Secure Practices: To mitigate risks, though no online system is entirely secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  1. Access and Correction: To access or update your personal information.
  2. Deletion: To request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
  3. Opt-Out: To opt out of marketing communications or disable cookies via browser settings.
