Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Bitforge Software Solutions, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our services, including API integrations, customizable plugins, and addon management tools. We aim to be transparent about our data practices and ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and any details submitted through our contact forms or when engaging with our services.
  • Usage Data: Non-personal information about your interactions with our website, such as IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site, collected via cookies and similar technologies.
  • Service-Related Data: Information provided during the use of our software solutions, such as configuration settings or preferences, to enhance your experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services, including responding to inquiries and delivering customized solutions.
  • Communicate with you about updates, promotions, or new features related to our products.
  • Analyze website usage to optimize performance and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and interests.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who assist in delivering our services (e.g., hosting or analytics providers), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Bitforge, our users, or the public.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data. However, no online transmission or storage method is 100% secure, and we encourage you to take precautions when sharing sensitive information.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@bitforge.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
